About

A note from the Lunawake team.

Hi,

Most sleep products start with data. We started with a feeling: the moment you’re almost asleep, and you don’t want to think, tap, or manage anything anymore.

Lunawake is built for that moment. It’s a bedside companion that quietly senses your body’s rhythm and adjusts what it does—so winding down feels natural, and waking up feels like a gentle return, not a jolt.

We believe better sleep isn’t about doing more. It’s about removing friction at the exact moments you’re most sensitive to it—when you’re letting go, and when you’re coming back.

That belief shapes everything we make:

We build interactions that are simple on the surface, but precise underneath. Lunawake can work without wearables, reading subtle body signals to time guidance and transitions. And in the morning, you can wake to the voice you want—including voice cloning—timed to your rhythm.

Just as important is what we won’t do. We’re designing Lunawake to feel safe in your home: calm, private, and predictable. The goal is trust you don’t have to think about.

If you ever meet us in person—at CES or anywhere else—you’ll notice we talk less about “features” and more about the edges: the exact second audio fades, the tone that doesn’t startle, the pace that respects your breathing. Those small decisions are where sleep becomes easier.

Thanks for being here. If you’re exploring Lunawake, we hope it feels like an invitation: relax—then let go.

— The Lunawake team
