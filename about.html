About Us

We’re Lunawake — Bedside Intelligence that helps you sleep better and wake up smarter. It supports the full journey from night to morning: tracking your sleep without wearables, waking you more gently at the right moment, and guiding small routines that help your schedule actually stick.

Lunawake sits on your nightstand and senses breathing and micro-movement, then uses that context to shape a more natural wake-up flow — less shock, less endless snoozing. And we built it around a simple truth: “wake-up time” doesn’t mean the same thing every day. Sometimes you need to get up now. Sometimes you want a little more sleep but can’t risk oversleeping. Sometimes you want to ease into clarity. Lunawake adapts to those real scenarios.

Our experience is guided by evidence-based sleep science, translated into habits you can actually follow. Over time, Lunawake becomes an AI coach that learns your rhythm and helps you improve it — steadily and without friction.

We’re a team of senior algorithm engineers, hardware engineers, and interaction designers. We’ve completed hundreds of hours of real-world testing, finished key validation through EVT/DVT, and raised our first round with support from a well-known VC firm and experienced investors.

Lunawake uses contactless mmWave radar to capture stable overnight signals without wearables and without cameras.
We’re planning to ship in June this year — with privacy-first design, ongoing updates, and transparent delivery.
