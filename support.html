FAQs

How does Dawn00's AI personalization work? ▼
Dawn00's AI learns from your sleep patterns, preferences, and daily routines to create a personalized wake-up experience. It adapts to your schedule and optimizes your wake-up time based on your sleep cycles.
What makes Dawn00 different from other alarm clocks? ▼
Dawn00 combines AI-driven personalization, voice wake-up technology, and sleep cycle optimization to create a gentle, natural wake-up experience tailored to your individual needs.
How do I set up my Dawn00 device? ▼
Setting up Dawn00 is simple: connect it to your Wi-Fi network, download the SYNC ART app, and follow the in-app setup guide to customize your wake-up experience.
Can I customize the wake-up sounds? ▼
Yes, Dawn00 offers a variety of natural sounds, music, and personalized voice messages. You can also record your own wake-up messages or choose from our curated collection.
What's included in the warranty? ▼
Dawn00 comes with a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects and hardware issues. Extended warranty options are available through our premium support plan.
