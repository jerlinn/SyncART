Your Guide to Lunawake

Basics

What is Lunawake? ▼

Lunawake is an AI bedside sleep mentor that guides how you fall asleep and wake up—through smart, adaptive interactions. We focus on a better wake-up routine.

Why is Lunawake different from a regular alarm clock? ▼

A regular alarm can only ring at a fixed time—it doesn’t handle the real gap between when you’d like to wake up and when you must wake up. Think about how you actually use your phone: multiple alarms, snoozing, half-awake moments, quick get-ups, then back to bed. Lunawake understands those choices and responds with a smart routine—not just noise. And because it tracks sleep automatically with no wearable, it can also guide you to fall asleep, improve your schedule over time, and make consistency finally feel easy.

What if I’m awake but not fully up? ▼

That’s exactly the point—Lunawake is designed for the “in-between” moments and responds with a guided routine.

How it Works

Does it still track sleep? ▼

Yes—mainly to understand context and patterns, not to show you endless metrics.

How does it work without wearables? ▼

It tracks breathing and movement contact-free with a bedside sensor—no ring, no watch

Can it wake two people? ▼

It works in a shared bed, but for now it tracks only the sleeper closest to the device

Privacy & Safety

Do you use a camera or keep voice recordings? ▼

A: No camera. And we only listen during a short “talk” window you trigger—no always-on mic, no stored raw audio.

Is it a medical device? ▼

No. It’s a consumer wellness product and not intended to diagnose, treat, or prevent disease.

App & Compatibility

Is there an app? ▼

Yes. You can use the app to set goals, configure routines, and view insights—but you don’t need it day to day. Lunawake works on its own as a full-featured alarm, and you can interact directly on the device.

Does it work without Wi-Fi? ▼

Core functions work locally. Some features like updates and backups need Wi-Fi.

What phones are supported? ▼

iOS and Android.

How long does personalization take? ▼

You’ll get value right away. Personalization improves over the first 1–2 weeks.
